This plugin allows you to display all your customers reviews on your WordPress site.

== Description ==

This plugin allows you to integrate the Begenius Reputationgenius system into your WordPress site and requires a Reputationgenius account.
If you don't have a Reputationgenius account yet, you can create one by visiting http://admin.reputationgenius.it.
Once the account is obtained, the plugin will preiodically download from our servers all the reviews available through your RSS feeds.
You will then be able to place your reviews on your pages using a shortcode.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `bg-reputationgenius.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Configure the plugin clicking on `Reputationgenius` and setting up the required options
1. Place the shortcode `[bgrg_comments]` on your pages.
